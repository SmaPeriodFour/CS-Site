Competitions


As a team we will participate in multiple competitions, which are listed below, as well as several competitions that can be done on your own time. For more information, click any image.




USACO (USA Computing Olympiad)


The USA Computing Olympiad is a national Computer Science Competition where teams have opportunities to compete three different times throughout the year. Those who do well may be selected to represent the United States overseas.



American Computer Science League


The ACSL is an annual competition divided into multiple divisions based on skill level, with tests ranging from knowledge of algorithms to problem solving with Python, Java, or C++.
